Other Historical Items

Space Cat Games Version II

There is an abandoned re-write of Space Cat Games called Space Cat Games Version II. It was maintained for a short period of 2 months, and was released once. It never went mainstream, and never replaced Space Cat Games Version 11 (latest at the time).

SPC Legacy

There is also a lost version of Space Cat Games Version 11, that was known internally as "SPC Legacy". It was to be released when Space Cat Games II went mainstream. It was never released, however, functions relating to SPC Legacy can be found in the code.
